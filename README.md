# 📌 Project Overview

The Insurance Data Analysis Dashboard project focuses on analyzing insurance policy, customer, claims, premium, and feedback data to uncover valuable business insights related to policy performance, customer behavior, claim trends, revenue generation.

This project was developed using Power BI to build an interactive and dynamic business intelligence dashboard that enables insurance stakeholders to monitor KPIs, evaluate claim performance, analyze customer demographics, and make data-driven business decisions.

The dashboard provides detailed analysis across multiple insurance categories such as Auto Insurance, Travel Insurance, Health Insurance, and other policy segments while also analyzing premium collection, claim settlement performance and policy coverage trends.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Insurance companies often struggle to monitor policy performance, track claim settlements, analyze customer feedback, and identify high-risk or high-value customer segments.

Traditional reporting methods make it difficult to:

• Analyze claim approval and rejection trends

• Monitor premium collection and revenue generation

• Compare insurance coverage against claims

• Evaluate policy-wise profitability and claim risk

• Generate real-time business insights for decision-making

This project solves these problems by creating a centralized Power BI dashboard with dynamic visualizations, drill-down analysis, and scheduled refresh functionality connected with SQL-based data sources.

## 📌 Dataset Details

### 1️⃣ Insurance Policy Data

The dataset contains 10,004 records with insurance-related information. It includes customer demographics, policy details, premium values, coverage, and claim information.

📑 Columns in the Dataset

• PolicyNumber – Unique identifier for each policy

• CustomerID – Unique customer identifier

• Gender – Customer gender

• Age – Customer age

• PolicyType – Type of insurance policy (e.g., Auto, Travel)

• PolicyStartDate – Policy start date

• PolicyEndDate – Policy end date

• PremiumAmount – Premium paid for the policy

• CoverageAmount – Total coverage amount

• ClaimNumber – Claim identifier

• ClaimDate – Date of claim (if any)

• ClaimAmount – Amount claimed

• ClaimStatus – Status of the claim (Approved, Pending, Rejected)
